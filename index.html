A little about me...

I am a Front-end Developer who specializes in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and React.js. My introduction to web development began in 2005. Now, coming up on two years, I have been taking coding more seriously by enrolling in courses online, networking with other developers, and building small-scale web applications. I am currently sharpening my front-end skills in the Full Stack Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin. I have built a number of projects that I am really proud of (see below) and am excited for what is to come!


You can check out some of the technologies I've learned from Code School, Team Treehouse, and Codecademy.
