About Isaac

I am a Front-end Developer who specializes in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and React. My introduction to web development began in 2005 via designing and customizing Wordpress and Xanga sites. Now, coming up on two years, I have been taking coding more seriously by enrolling in courses online, networking with other developers, and building small-scale web applications. I am currently sharpening my front-end skills in the Full Stack Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin. I have built a number of projects that I am really proud of (see below) and am excited for what is to come!
